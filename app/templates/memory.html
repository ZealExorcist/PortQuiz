How to Play

Find matching pairs of ports and protocols. Turn over two cards at a time. If they match (a port and its corresponding protocol), you score a point and the cards stay face up. If they don't match, they turn back over.

In two-player mode, players take turns. The player with the most pairs at the end wins!
